Catch Help

Favorite

Add this Catch or Fishing Spot to your list of Favorites for quick access.

Measure Later

Swipe to the photo that you took with your fish lying next to a known object to use the innovative Measure Later system.

Share

Share your Catch to Instagram, Facebook or where ever you want and you can share as much or as little of the information as you want.

For Fishing Spots you can share the location with a friend or get driving directions from where ever you are to that spot!

Edit

Edit the place name, weight or length. Refresh the satellite image. Refresh the weather.

Trash

Delete this Catch or Spot.

Photo Options

If you took multiple photos for a catch, hold your finger on one of the photos to either set that photo as the default, or to delete it.
