Map Help

The Map allows you to see where your catches where, where your fishing spots are and where you got skunked (hopefully never).
Use the top bar to switch between viewing just Catches, Spots, Skunks or viewing All. The two buttons on the right side toggle between satellite view and map view.
Tap on any of the icons on the map to popup it's name - then tap on the information button to go to the details.
If you own the Pro Version you can hold your finger at a location on the map and create a new Fishing Spot right there. So, you can use the map to search for new hidden spots and mark them right in Fishie! Then next time your out - check Fishie to see if you're near any fishing holes.
Tip: When you go to the details on a Fishing Spot tap the share button to get driving directions to that spot, or you can send the Location URL to one of your fishing buddies.
