Map Help

Icons

Use the top-bar buttons to view Catches , Spots , Skunked or all map icons. Tap on any of the icons on the map to popup it's name - then tap on the information button to go to the details.

Create new Spots and Catches

If you own the Pro Version - hold your finger at a location on the map to create a new Fishing Spot or a Catch. Use the map to search for new hidden spots and mark them right in Fishie! Then next time your out - check Fishie to see if you're near any fishing holes.

Driving Directions

When you go to the details on a Fishing Spot tap the share button to get driving directions to that spot, or you can send the Location URL to one of your fishing buddies.
