About Fishie

Hello! Fishie was developed by me, Jef Armstrong, out of my love for fishing. I started with the simple idea of creating a better camera for fish pictures and the app grew out of that. As I "field tested" Fishie (that's what you get to call 'going fishing' when you are developing a fishing app), I added features that me and my fishing buddies needed.

Thanks
