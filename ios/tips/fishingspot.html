Fishing Spots

Create A Spot

You can create a spot by either taking a picture here, or by holding your finger at a spot on the map.

Weather

Once a spot is created view the details to see a quick weather statement, or tap the sun button to see a detailed weather report.

Directions / Share

Tap the share button to get driving directions to the spot or to share the spot with one of your fishing buddies.
