Camera Tips

Volume Buttons

Volume Up to snap a photo.
Volume Down to switch cameras.

Mutiple Photos for Catch

Take as many photos as you want - they're all saved together as one catch.

Photo Originals

Every original full sized photo is saved to your photos library - just like if you used the regular camera. You can turn this off in Settings if you like.

Release

Tap the Next button in the upper right or any of the thumbnails when you're all done.

Pro Tip

Take a photo of your fish with a known object in the photo (like your rod) in order to measure the fish later!Pro Version
