Camera Tips

Use the Volume Up button to snap a photo.

Use the Volume Down button to switch between the front and back cameras.

Take a photo of your fish with a known object in the photo (like your rod) in order to measure the fish later!Pro Version

Take as many photos as you want - they're all saved together as One catch. You can always delete the ones you don't like.

Tap the Next button in the top right when you're all done.
